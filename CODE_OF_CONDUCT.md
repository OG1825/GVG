# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

GVG is committed to providing an inclusive, respectful, and welcoming environment for all members of our community – whether builders, mentors, reviewers, or partners.

## Our Values

### 🎯 Meritocratic
The best work wins. Judging is fair, transparent, and based on merit. We do not play favourites.

### 🤝 Supportive
Everyone starts somewhere. We help each other succeed. Feedback is constructive, not harsh.

### 📚 Growth-Focused
GVG exists to help people learn, grow, and build. Failure is a learning opportunity, not a shame.

### 🔒 Integrity-First
We do the right thing, always. No shortcuts. No politics. Honest communication.

### 🌍 Inclusive
Everyone is welcome. Different backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives make us stronger.

### ⚡ Execution-Driven
We ship real projects. We celebrate done over perfect.

## Expected Behaviour

All community members agree to:

- **Be respectful**: Treat everyone with dignity. Disagreement is welcome; disrespect is not.
- **Be honest**: Share your thoughts openly and listen to others. No hidden agendas.
- **Be helpful**: Offer support, feedback, and knowledge freely. Help others succeed.
- **Be inclusive**: Welcome different perspectives. Create space for quieter voices.
- **Take responsibility**: Own your mistakes and learn from them. Don't blame others.
- **Be professional**: Represent GVG with pride. We are building something serious.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

Behaviour that will not be tolerated:

- **Harassment**: Bullying, intimidation, or abuse of any kind. This includes sexism, racism, or discrimination.
- **Plagiarism**: Submitting others' work as your own. Always give credit.
- **Bad faith**: Intentionally sabotaging others or the community. Cheating on bounties.
- **Exclusion**: Deliberately making others feel unwelcome based on identity or background.
- **Pressure tactics**: Forcing others to agree with you or silencing dissent.

## Enforcement

Violations of this code will be taken seriously. Consequences may include:

1. **First violation**: Private warning and clear explanation of the issue.
2. **Second violation**: Temporary suspension from bounties and community (1–4 weeks).
3. **Serious or repeated violations**: Permanent removal from GVG.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience a violation, report it privately:

- **Email**: [conduct@gvg.example.com](mailto:conduct@gvg.example.com)
- **Discord DM**: Message a community moderator
- **Anonymous form**: [response.form/gvg-report](https://response.form/gvg-report)

**All reports are confidential.** We will not share your identity without permission.

## Questions?

Unsure about what this means? Ask. We're here to help, not to gotcha you.

---

**By joining GVG, you agree to uphold this code. Thank you for helping build a community we're proud of.**
