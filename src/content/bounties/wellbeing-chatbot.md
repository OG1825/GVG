## Overview

A supportive, AI-powered chatbot providing 24/7 initial mental health support and resource navigation for students. The system connects users with professional services whilst offering immediate coping strategies and information.

## Completed Features

✅ Natural language understanding for mental health topics  
✅ 24/7 availability with escalation to human counsellors  
✅ Resource matching (therapists, crisis services, support groups)  
✅ Anonymous usage option  
✅ HIPAA-compliant conversation logging  
✅ Multi-language support (5 languages)  
✅ Mobile app integration  

## Impact

- **1,247** students supported since launch
- **94%** positive satisfaction rating
